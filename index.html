A little bit About Kai'sa

Kai’Sa is a human born in the desert of Shurima who was trapped in the depths of the hellish realm called the Void. While there she encountered a certain Voidling that got a little touchy-feely, establishing a symbiotic relationship with the girl. She is now melded with the organism like a biosuit, granting her powers similar to the Voidborn monsters that inhabit the realm. Her name resembles Finnish Kaisa, which is derived from Pre-Greek Αἰκατερίνη (Aikaterine).In-game, Kai'Sa can toggle her helmet on and off [default: Ctrl + 5].Kai'Sa's symbiote-suit adapts to her chosen attack style.

Kai'sa Pros and Cons on history

  • Feats: Kai’Sa has survived in the Void for a full decade, killing Voidborn and Voidlings throughout that time period. She can obliterate sizable Void creatures with a single shot, use her plasma barrage to obliterate many Voidlings at once, and did both with deadly accuracy.

  • Weaknesses:Kai’Sa does rely on the Voidling biosuit for her power, meaning if you can find a way to kill the suit, you probably kill Kai’Sa. In addition she is still human under that suit, and being able to overpower her defenses would likely spell doom for the Void huntress.
Manfred Alonso
